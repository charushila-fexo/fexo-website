THE FEXO STORY

Bridging the Intelligence Gap

Fexo was founded to solve a paradox: financial institutions are data-rich but insight-poor. Critical decisions – from lending to audits - are often paralyzed by the sheer volume and complexity of unstructured documents. We recognized that generic AI models, prone to hallucination and lacking context, were not the answer for an industry built on precision.

We took a different path. We built FexoLM, a proprietary, sovereign agentic AI trained explicitly on the DNA of Indian financial, commercial and legal documents. We exist to turn the chaos of non-standard financial statements, handwritten notes, or vernacular deeds into trusted, actionable data. We do not just build software; we build the infrastructure for faster, safer financial inclusion.

Backed by Visionaries

We are funded by a strategic group of private investors who recognize that the credit gap and insurance claim delays are fundamentally information gaps. Their capital and mentorship validate our mission: Fexo is not just a tool; we are solving genuine, structural problems in the economy.
