THE FEXO STORY

Built for the Hardest Problems

Founded in 2024, Fexo was born from a simple observation: the most critical decisions in finance, lending, and insurance were still bottlenecked by manual, error-prone paperwork.

Generic AI couldn't understand the nuance of a non-MCA financial statement or a handwritten FIR. We built FexoLM, our **proprietary agentic AI**, to solve this specific, high-value problem. Today, we are a dedicated team driven by a single goal: to build the most intelligent, reliable, and secure automation platform for BFSI and Audit.
