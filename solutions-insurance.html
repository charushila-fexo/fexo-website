THE PROBLEM

Slow, Expensive, and Vulnerable to Fraud

Claims processing is slow and expensive. It is bogged down by manual data entry from diverse, unstructured documents (handwritten forms, vernacular reports, photos). This makes it difficult to spot fraud and creates a poor policyholder experience.

THE SOLUTION

The Co-pilot for Claims Adjudication

Fexo's Co-pilot reads and understands everything. It digitizes handwritten text, authenticates data by cross-referencing all documents, and provides a clear summary with a recommendation: "Process for STP*" or "Flag for Human Review".

*STP: Straight Through Processing
