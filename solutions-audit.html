THE PROBLEM

High-Risk, Time-Consuming Manual Audits

Manual auditing is a high-stakes, time-consuming process. Auditors must cross-reference thousands of pages across multiple years, checking for compliance with complex standards.

This traditional method is high-risk, unscalable, and prone to human error, leading to missed non-compliance issues and significant operational bottlenecks.

THE SOLUTION

A Force-Multiplier for Audit Teams

Fexo's Co-pilot acts as a powerful force-multiplier for audit teams. It ingests multi-year financial reports, ITRs, and even vernacular documents to perform a comprehensive review in minutes.

Our AI flags every anomaly, non-compliance issue, or high-risk transaction with an evidence-based link, turning months of manual work into an automated, auditable, and accurate process.
