THE PROBLEM

Slow, Risky, and Unscalable

Manual credit analysis is slow, risky, and unscalable. Lenders spend hours per case manually reading annual reports, bank statements, GST statements, ITRs and other lending related documents. This creates bottlenecks, increases risk, and leads to a slow customer experience.

THE SOLUTION

The Co-pilot for Business and Corporate Lending

Fexo's Co-pilot automates the entire process. It ingests all documents (even non-MCA format annual reports, scanned Bank and GST statements), performs quantitative and qualitative analysis, monitors external risk, and generates a fully-auditable Credit Assessment Memo in minutes, not hours.
